How to make an Tuxedo Mocha!

Assembly time: 10 minutes
Awesome rating: 100% awesome

Are you wondering how to make tasty coffee? Why don't you try to make it yourself? Follow the steps and you'll make a great iced mocha!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Mixing cup
  • Chocolate milk
  • White chocolate sauce
  • Chocolate sauce
  • Esspresso shot
  • Ice

  1. Grab one mix cup and one cup of ice.

    Set them to the side so that you can grab them easily later.

  2. Grab your chocolate sauce, and your white chocolate sauce, and meaure out a spoonful of each into your mix tin.

    You can add more or less flavor depending on how sweet you'd like your coffee.

  3. Pull a shot of expresso.

    After the shot is pulled, mix it with the flavors.

  4. Measure out half a cup of chocolate milk.

    If you'd like to make this drink hot, steam the milk.

  5. Mix all of the ingrediants together and pour over ice.

    Congratulations! You've made a Tuxedo Mocha .
